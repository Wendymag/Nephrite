Our Background

Nephrite was established in 2014 with a particular initial focus on up recruitment and also offering skills for the hospitality industry. After achieving success in this industry, Nephrite broadened its recruitment profile and now focuses on proactively sourcing from high caliber executives, senior and middle managers, professionals to entry-level positions, across all business disciplines and industry categories. Driven by our passion for people, our philosophy is to develop close long - term people focused partnerships with clients and applicants in order to provide complete hospitality staffing solutions across industry. (Nephrite) specializes in assisting caterers, Restaurants, Hotels and organizations with finding the best human resource solutions for their specific needs. We are experienced consulting and development practitioners who specialize in optimizing the capacity of people in both the private and public sectors. Our focus is on providing best- practice recruitment and placement solutions that enhance both individual and organizational competence. We recruit and place in terms of the specific needs of our clients, and our interventions are therefore outcomes-based, focusing on the identification of skills, knowledge, attributes and attitudes. We feel that our interventions surpass the traditional approach, by focusing on all related individual and organisational aspects both prior to, and after delivering tailored solutions.

Our Methology

(Nephrite) develops customized solutions. We start by working with the client to fully understand the client's needs. We use a number of assessment tools to provide insights and information on the organization, and bring the specific employment need into context with the broader organizational needs. Then, together with the client, we develop an appropriate job profile for the specific vacancy before we identify and execute the most appropriate recruitment strategy. Furthermore, we continue working with the client to evaluate the impact and success of our placements as well as strengthen client relations on an ongoing basis. Chefs 7. Kitchen Assistance 8. House Keeping
